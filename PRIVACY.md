# Privacy Policy

## We do not collect any personal information

AI Food Mixer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

All data — including your creations, custom ingredients, and settings — is stored locally on your device using SwiftData.

AI-generated content is produced entirely on-device using Apple's Foundation Models and Image Playground. No data is sent to external servers.
